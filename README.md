> **This pack does not take compliance reviews into account.** Get your own compliance counsel and partner bank marketing review before publishing anything generated by these skills.

## Who this is for

Marketers, growth leads, and founders at B2C neobanks and challenger banks. Built with Chime, Cash App, Revolut, Varo, Current, Dave, SoFi, Step, Monzo, N26, and Greenwood in mind. Useful for any consumer-facing fintech that runs on a partner-bank model and earns interchange revenue.

If you market a B2B fintech, business banking, or a non-deposit product, this pack is not your fit. Use a SaaS pack instead.

## Why this pack exists (first principles)

Generic SaaS marketing skills miss the point of how neobanks actually grow. A clever onboarding hook that improves activation in a SaaS funnel can be the wrong play in a banking funnel. A "limited time" growth tactic borrowed from a B2B playbook can hurt more than it helps when the product is a deposit account.

Neobank marketing has its own physics. The entire unit economics model rests on interchange revenue from card swipes, with secondary revenue from net interest margin, lending products, and subscription tiers. Direct deposit routing is the single best predictor of lifetime value. Every marketing decision should be evaluated against one question: does this move users toward direct-deposit-routed primary card use?

This pack derives every skill from one funnel:

Awareness, Sign-up (KYC), First deposit, Direct deposit routed, Card-as-primary, Revenue feature engagement, Referral.
